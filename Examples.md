# Dynamic Evidence Weighting

The AI Agent adjusts evidence importance according to asset characteristics.

Example:

### Large-Cap Assets

Higher importance is given to:

- Open Interest
- Funding Rate
- Liquidity

### Small-Cap Assets

Higher importance is given to:

- Capital Commitment
- Whale Activity
- Token Events

This adaptive weighting allows AI Evidence Engine to evaluate different market structures without relying on fixed parameters.

# Risk Reminder

AI Evidence Engine is a decision-support framework.

Unexpected macroeconomic events, exchange incidents, security breaches, regulatory announcements, or sudden market volatility may invalidate current evidence.

Users should always combine AI-generated recommendations with proper risk management before executing trades.
